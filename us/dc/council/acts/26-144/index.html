Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget Emergency Act of 2025.

AN ACT

To adopt, on an emergency basis, the local portion of the budget of the District of Columbia government for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may be cited as the "Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget Emergency Act of 2025".

Sec. 2. Adoption of the local portion of the Fiscal Year 2026 budget.

The following expenditure levels are adopted pursuant to section 446(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 801; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.46(a)), as the local portion of the budget for the government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026.
